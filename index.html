Outsider MC Angeles City Chapter ......

 

 

........ opened on the 1st July 2016 by some of the officers and members of the Laguna Chapter who resided in Angeles City and Marinduque.

 

It was felt at that time to be the appropriate time to expand the Outsider MC brotherhood in the Philippines and open an OMC Angeles City Chapter.

 

Although this is a newly formed Chapter its officers and members are long standing residents of the Philippines of over 20 years or more and well known in the motorcycle community of the Philippines.

 

The new Chapter is formed with officers and members with various nationalities, Austrian, Scottish, Australian, and German and hope to attract expat foreigners and local Filipino riders who meet the criteria and enjoy the brotherhood and comradery associated with Motorcycle riding in the Philippines.
