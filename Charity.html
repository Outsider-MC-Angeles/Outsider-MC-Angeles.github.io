Charity

On the streets of Angeles City, a medical emergency unfolded. A very sick, dehydrated, and malnourished three-year-old boy was found, his name is "little John". Thankfully, a kind stranger rushed him to the hospital. The boy's condition was dire, and without immediate help, he might not have survived another week. He was not only suffering from severe malnutrition and dehydration but also battling tuberculosis and pneumonia. His mother, unable to care for him, left him vulnerable and alone. Once he is stable, he will be transferred to the Baptist Orphanage Ministry.


The Baptist Orphanage Ministry, established by Pastor Jeremy Ferguson, is located just 20 minutes outside of Angeles City. Here, many children from the squatter areas find refuge and a new chance at life. Pastor Jeremy, along with his family, lives on-site, dedicating their lives to caring for these children.


During our recent visit to the orphanage, the story of this little boy deeply touched us. We are determined to provide regular support to the orphanage and help these children thrive.


We are reaching out to our compassionate friends, club members , prospects and hangarounds to join us in this effort. Currently, donations can be made via GCash or in person. Our goal is to make our first donation of rice by the end of June. Each 25kg bag of rice costs around PHP 1200, and every contribution, no matter how small, will make a significant difference.
