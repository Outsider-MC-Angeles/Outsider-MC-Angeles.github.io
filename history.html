History

 

In 1973, the Satana MC disbanded in Innsbruck. The former members of this club formed two new MCs: the "MC Riders Of Limited" and the "Outsider MC".

As their patch, they chose the "ultimate outsider", a combination of an alien and an Indian. This emblem still adorns the vests of the members to this day. The patch also featured the club name in black and red font on a white background. The colors of this inscription were changed in 1983 to black font on a white background. In 2001, the inscriptions were revised again, adopting a more rounded and modern form.

In 1981, the first chapter in Germany, the Rosenheim Chapter, was established, which lasted until 1983. From 1987 to 1993, the Graz Chapter (Styria) also existed.

 

On May 1, 1982, the Vienna Chapter was founded, which is now the "Main Chapter". On February 16, 2002, the Eagles MC joined as the Eisenstadt Chapter, the Dragon Rider MC as the St. Pölten Chapter, the Soldiers MC as the Prospect Chapter Schwechat, and the Gallows Birds MC as the Prospect Chapters Mödling and Prospect Chapter Nomad Germany. In May 2002, the first chapter was also established on another continent: the Outsider MC is now represented with 5 chapters in the Philippines and 2 chapters in Thailand in Southeast Asia.

 

The Outsider MC is a Onepercenter club! We follow our own path and stand on only one side - ours! The club has become the majority of each of our members' lives and serves as a brotherhood and family. We live by our motto "One World - One Vision" and also maintain contacts with many clubs in Europe and overseas.
