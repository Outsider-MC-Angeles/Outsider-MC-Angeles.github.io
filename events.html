June 15-17, 2024

 

Memorial Ride

 

a. This will be an open ride for passengers riding on the back. We will journey to Batangas and then take a ferry to Puerto Galera, Mindoro. We plan to spend two nights in Puerto Galera, arriving on Saturday, June 15th, and departing on Monday, June 17th. Our goal is to catch the 10 am ferry departing from Batangas port on June 15th, arriving in Puerto Galera by noon.

 

b. The return trip to Angeles will be determined once we are in Puerto Galera.

 

c. Dave has already reserved four apartments with two bedrooms each at the Jack Daniels Hotel in Puerto Galera to accommodate eight people.
